Terēze Medne, born in 1995 in Riga. Currently studying in the 3rd year of the Visual Communication Department of the Latvian Academy of Arts. Works in various techniques and platforms, does documentary photography. In her works, she reflects the seemingly self-evident everyday life in a mixed technique in various installations.
